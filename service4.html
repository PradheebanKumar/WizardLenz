Wings of Wisdom

Wings of Wisdom is dedicated to driving social awareness and skill development in emerging technologies. Through targeted awareness campaigns and costfree skill development initiatives, we aim to educate and inspire individuals, organizations about the potential and impact of Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Mixed Reality (MR), Metaverse and Artificial Intelligence (AI). We believe in equipping communities with the knowledge to navigate and thrive in a tech-driven future.

Arcane Outreach

Drive awareness of emerging technologies and their potential impact through engaging campaigns that educate and inspire various audiences,using compelling content and interactive formats to highlight their benefits and real-world applications

SkillSorcery

Empower individuals with the skills needed for the future through targeted initiatives that focus on mastering Virtual Reality , Augmented Reality , Mixed Reality , Metaverse and Artificial Intelligence and other transformative technologies.
