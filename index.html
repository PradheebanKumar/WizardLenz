Starlight Scholars Guild

The Starlight Scholars Guild offers tailored training programs in cutting-edge technologies like AR, VR, Mixed Reality, Metaverse and Artificial Intelligence for schools, colleges, and corporate professionals. Our programs are designed to empower learners with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital landscape. From young students to seasoned professionals, we provide comprehensive, hands-on learning experiences that bridge the gap between education and industry needs.

Young Sorcerers' Academy

Empower young minds with foundational knowledge in emerging technologies like Virtual Reality , Augmented Reality , Mixed Reality , Metaverse, and Artificial Intelligence , fostering creativity and innovation from an early age.

Arcane Collegiate

Educating graduate students in advanced immersive technologies involves equipping them with specialized skills and knowledge to prepare them for successful careers in innovative and cutting-edge industries.

Corporate Enchanters

Provide specialized training in Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Mixed Reality (MR), Metaverse, and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to industry professionals, enhancing their capabilities to lead technological transformations within their organizations.
